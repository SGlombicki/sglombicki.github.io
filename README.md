# Welcome to my Personal Site
I am a high school student with a strong interest in cyber security and operational technology, I have dedicated myself to learning about and understanding the various concepts and technologies related to
this field. I have taken various online courses and completed relevant coursework in order to gain a strong foundation in this area. I have gotten my CCNA and CvCISO certification to help gain a broad understanding of the cyber space. I have taken great interest in ICS and SCADA systems and have taken and passed both Idaho National Lab course on securing ICS Systems. At home I have been building both a AD and Virtualization homelab along side my ICS homelab. In addition I compete in many cyber completions including CyberPatriot where I rank as a top 100 team and CTFs where I have placed first nationally. My CTF team ranks top 100 in the US.
